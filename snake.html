Snake Game

Control the snake and eat as many apples as you can!

Score: 0
High Score: 0

Instructions:

Use the arrow keys to control the snake:

  • ↑ - Move Up
  • ↓ - Move Down
  • ← - Move Left
  • → - Move Right

Eat the red apples to grow and increase your score!!!

The game ends if the snake hits the wall or itself.
